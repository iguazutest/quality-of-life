IS STABLE GOOD ENOUGH?

Patient quotes from a UK qualitative survey based study about the impact of cGVHD run with the collaboration of Anthony Nolan1,a

Swipe through these quotes to find out more.
When the GvHD was at its worst with my lungs I could barely walk maybe 50 metres before I was having to rest and in discomfort.
I was a teacher but I just had to give up due to the infection risk and I was too breathless. Any infection could take three, four weeks to recover from. So I had to stop.
People don’t come around so often now; they don’t know how to deal with it...
It’s tough, there’s no end in sight. It’s just relentless... I have to keep going for the kids, but sometimes you feel like you are going mad.
I am lucky I am not older and don’t have my own family to support. I couldn’t have done it.
I feel like we have to deal with their anxiety of infecting us. We just have to meet in the garden. We don’t really see them in the wintertime.
